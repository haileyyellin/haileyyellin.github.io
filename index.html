Hi! My name is Hailey Yellin, and I attend The University of Maryland studying sports journalism, class of 2024. In my life, I have lived in Maryland, Massachusetts, Tennessee, Illinois, and now back in Maryland. Although these places are so different, they have all inspired one thing inside me, sports. I have played almost any sport imaginable, from field hockey to soccer to basketball. With my love of sports and my passion toward media, I am excited to begin my career in sports media.
